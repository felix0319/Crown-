Our Journey

A timeline of excellence spanning over a decade.

2009 - Foundation

Crown Heritage was founded with the School of Nursing and 50 pioneering students.

2012 - Accreditation

Full accreditation received from the NMCN and PCN for our foundational programs.

2015 - Expansion

Opening of the modern multi-billion naira Research and Diagnostics Center.

2019 - Degree Awarding Status

Transitioned into a degree-awarding college in partnership with leading global universities.

2023 - Digital Transformation

Launched the Digital Campus initiative and AI-driven health learning portal.
