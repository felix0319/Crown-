Welcome to Crown Heritage

Founded in 2009, Crown Heritage College of Health has grown into a premier institution for healthcare education. We are dedicated to providing an environment that fosters academic excellence, professional development, and community service.

Our mission is to produce highly skilled healthcare professionals who are ethically sound and internationally competitive. With modern laboratories, a state-of-the-art library, and partnerships with leading hospitals, we ensure our students get the best practical exposure.
