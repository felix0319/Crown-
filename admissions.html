Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

Applicants must possess at least 5 O'Level credits (WAEC/NECO/NABTEB) including English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, obtained at not more than two sittings.

Direct Entry Applicants

Candidates with OND/HND/B.Sc. in relevant health fields can apply for direct entry into the 200 or 300 level, depending on the program and credential grade.
